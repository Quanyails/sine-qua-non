# Sunset Boulevard

## Design notes

Theme attempts:

- Move synonyms for JOURNEY to the start of each entry, making weird and funny phrases.
  - Reason for scrapping: Couldn't find enough synonyms for JOURNEY that made fun phrases.
- Move the word TRIP across entries from right to left, in phrases such as LET I(T RIP), PINS(TRIP)E SUITS, and (TRIP)LE CROWN.
  - Reason for scrapping: Couldn't find enough compelling phrases that include the substring TRIP without duplicating words like TRIPLE or STRIP/STRIPE.
- Move the letters T, R, I, and P across entries from right to left, such as LET I(T RIP), (TR)ADER SH(IP), and (TRIP)LE CROWN.
  - Reason for scrapping: Couldn't find compelling enough entries for each of the "movements".
- Create phrases where synonyms for JOURNEY are placed reversed in the string (AKA they are pointed "to the west"), like PO(KER T)ABLE.
  - Reason for scrapping: Couldn't find compelling enough entries for such synonyms.
- Include time zones in the order EASTERN, CENTRAL, MOUNTAIN, and PACIFIC.
  - Reason for scrapping: Felt too loosely connected to the revealer, as it doesn't mesh that time zones would take a journey.
