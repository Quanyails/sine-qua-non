# Crossword Resources

Last updated: 2026-03-27

## Construction

Guides:

- [Crossword Construction Basics](https://b.link/BrodyPuzBasics) by Sam Brody: Crossword construction guide—take with a grain of salt. Reservations:
  - Provides sample grids that would be cut off with a single added block.
  - Suggests using autofill to fill in a grid and editing it as you go along as a tenable construction strategy.
  - Doesn't make any mention of Crossword Nexus's word list.
  - Fixates too much on Crosserville.
  - Downplays Ingrid's features (i.e. performance + version control functionality).
- ⭐ [Crossword Constructor's Handbook](https://aframegames.com/store/?download=21): How-to book on the art of crossword construction ([source](https://www.facebook.com/groups/cruciverb/posts/1318988648792956/)).
- [How to Make a Crossword Puzzle: The Series](https://www.nytimes.com/2018/09/14/crosswords/how-to-make-a-crossword-puzzle-the-series.html): Guide on how to make crossword puzzles.
- ⭐ [OLLI Course: Constructing Crosswords](https://communicrossings.com/olli-course-constructing-crosswords)
- [Random House Puzzlemaker's Handbook](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0812925440) ([source](https://www.cruciverb.com/index.php?action=ezportal;sa=page;p=21)): Old book with a section on American-style crossword construction.

## Info lookup

- [All the downloadable word lists I've been able to find](https://www.reddit.com/comments/nqsuku/): Crossword word lists.
- [ConceptNet](https://conceptnet.io/): Find word associations.
- [Datamuse API](https://www.datamuse.com/api/): Find words using semantic lookup.
- [Find Quotes](https://www.goodreads.com/quotes/search?q=): Quotations lookup.
- [Genius](https://genius.com/): Song/lyric lookup.
- [Idioms and phrases](https://idioms.thefreedictionary.com/): Dictionary of idioms and phrases.
- [Nearest words](https://www.hakank.org/nearest_words/): Find words by edit distance.
- [Nutrimatic](https://nutrimatic.org/): Advanced phrase search tool.
- ⭐ [OneLook Dictionary Search and Thesaurus](https://onelook.com/): Advanced term lookup.
- [Quotes and Famous Sayings](https://www.quotationspage.com/): Quotations lookup.
- [Roget's Thesaurus](https://www.gutenberg.org/files/10681/10681-h/10681-h.htm): Advanced word lookup.
- ⭐ [TV Tropes](https://tvtropes.org/): Look up terms in pop culture.
- ⭐ [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special:Search): Search terms for alternate cluing angles.
  - `intitle:"keyword"`: Search article titles by keyword.
- ⭐ [Wikipedia Regex Search](https://crosswordnexus.com/wiki/): Wrapper for searching Wikipedia articles with scores. Good for finding terms and/or cluing angles.
